For this reason we use the published content to provide various services to institutions, companies, and other organizations. Those services are socially oriented and further help bettering the environment we all live in. For example, we generate a lot of reports for traffic violations which are done by drivers with company branded cars. We provide companies with a monitoring service with which they receive instant notifications when one of their cars is uploaded in “Citizens” violating the rules. By having this information companies can take measures within their own business environment. Thus, to impact positively traffic. We do not provide your personal data to those companies, but only the data regarding the traffic violation. Another service that we provide is a software-as-a-service for institutions in the platform. Authorities can log in and process your report/requests. By using it they cut cost and become more effective in solving social problems.
